Paneer

Paneer or cheese is nothing but a combination of carbohydrates, fats and proteins. 100 gm of paneer contains few carbs, 8gm of protein and 8 gm of fats (saturated). So neither it's a rich source of protein nor fat.

Pani Puri

Panipuri or Phuchka is a type of snack that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It consists of a round or ball-shaped, hollow puri, filled with a mixture of flavored water, tamarind chutney, chili, chaat masala, potato, onion or chickpeas.

Idly

Idli or idly are a type of savoury rice cake, originating from the Indian subcontinent, popular as breakfast foods in Southern India and among Tamils in Sri Lanka.
